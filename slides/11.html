What is a MobiCryp HUB?

A HUB is your personal crypto minting node that runs on MobiCryp's infrastructure. Think of it as your digital money factory that generates returns through stablecoin minting and arbitrage. The HUB price (in TRDO) is a one-time subscription fee that determines your USDT capacity.

Important: The HUB itself is an expense (subscription). Your ROI is generated only from the USDT amount you mint, not from the HUB price.

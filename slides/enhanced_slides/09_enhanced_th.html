WHAT YOU'RE BUYING

Understanding the two key components of the MobiCryp system

HUB

Your Access Point to the Platform

  • One-time TRDO fee

    This is the system access fee (not part of your ROI calculation)

  • Range from $55 to $1.1M

    Different hub levels have different capacity limits

  • Unlocks platform features

    Required to participate in both investing and team building

Entry Example:

$55 Hub Fee

CAPACITY

Your Earning Potential

  • USDT deposited to earn

    This is the investment amount that earns 250% ROI

  • Limited by hub size

    Each hub has a maximum capacity it can hold

  • Can be added gradually

    Start small and add capacity as your confidence grows

Entry Example:

$165 - $330 USDT

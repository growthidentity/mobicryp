How Binary Bonus Works

1
Your organization is divided into two teams: LEFT and RIGHT
2
Both legs accumulate volume from all team purchases
3
You earn 10% bonus on the volume of your weaker leg
4
Example: Left = $8,000 / Right = $5,000
5
Your bonus = 10% of $5,000 = $500

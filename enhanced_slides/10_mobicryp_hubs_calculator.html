What is a MobiCryp HUB?

A HUB is your personal crypto minting node that runs on MobiCryp's infrastructure. Think of it as your digital money factory that generates returns through stablecoin minting and arbitrage.

Activation Requirement: To activate a HUB, you must mint a minimum of 3x the HUB price. For example, a 55 USDT HUB requires at least 165 USDT of minting.
